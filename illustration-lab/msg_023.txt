Second, and this is the real one. You were right that keep or skip only asks whether the moment is good, never whether the drawing is good. So now there are three choices. Keep means the moment and the drawing both work. Skip means not this moment at all. And the new middle one, redraw, means the moment is great but the picture needs changing. When you pick redraw, it asks you what should change, and you can type a quick note, like the cars needing something that shows identity. Those go into a separate redraw queue at the end.

So a good idea with a wrong drawing doesn't get thrown away anymore. It gets fixed. Take it for a spin and tell me how the redraw flow feels.
