Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile apps published by Sage Ryza ("we," "us") — including Little Book of Miracles, Side Quest, and our other apps (each, an "App") — handle your information. It applies to all of our Apps; some sections apply only to Apps that offer the feature described.

Information we handle

  • Content you create. Things you write or add in an App — journal entries, captions, photos you choose, quest progress, profile details. This is stored so the App works for you.
  • An anonymous account identifier. Several Apps sign you in anonymously (no name, email, or password) so your content stays tied to your device. We do not require a personal account to use these Apps.
  • Basic diagnostics. Standard, non-identifying technical information needed to operate and debug the App.

We do not sell your information, and we do not use it for third-party advertising or cross-app tracking.

AI features and third-party AI providers

Some Apps offer optional AI features (for example, turning a journal entry in Little Book of Miracles into an illustration). When — and only when — you use such a feature, the content you submit for it (such as the text you wrote) is sent to our AI providers to generate your result:

  • Anthropic (Claude) — to turn your words into a drawing description.
  • OpenAI — to generate text, prompts, or images for some creations.
  • Replicate — to generate the image from that description.

This data is used solely to produce the result you asked for. We ask for your explicit consent inside the App before any content is sent to these providers for the first time, and you can decline and keep using the App without the AI feature. Each provider processes the data under its own terms and privacy policy.

Service providers

We use Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Storage, and Hosting) to run our Apps and store your content securely. Generated images are stored in Firebase Storage.

Keeping and deleting your data

Your content stays until you delete it in the App or ask us to remove it. To request deletion of data associated with your device, email us at the address below and we will help.

Children

Our Apps are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Changes

We may update this policy; we will revise the "Last updated" date above when we do.
